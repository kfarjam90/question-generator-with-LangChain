## MCQ 1
Question: What is art considered to be, according to the text, beyond just its physical forms?
A) A form of entertainment
B) A means of financial gain
C) The expression of the human experience
D) A way to showcase technical skill
Correct Answer: C) The expression of the human experience

## MCQ 2
Question: What is at the core of art, according to the text?
A) Technical skill
B) Financial gain

Correct Answer: C) A form of communication
